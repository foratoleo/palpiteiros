## üîç Quick Reference

### Key Concepts

**Prediction Markets**: Markets where traders buy and sell shares based on the probability of future events.

**Share Prices**: Prices range from $0.00 to $1.00 USDC, representing the probability of an event occurring.

**Market Resolution**: When a market closes, shares of the correct outcome are worth $1.00 each.

**Order Types**:
- **Market Orders**: Execute immediately at current market price
- **Limit Orders**: Execute only at your specified price or better

### Rewards Programs

- **Holding Rewards**: Earn rewards for holding positions in markets
- **Liquidity Rewards**: Earn rewards for providing liquidity to the orderbook
- **Maker Rebates**: Reduced fees for market makers who provide liquidity

### Trading Fees

Polymarket offers competitive trading fees with rebates for market makers. See [Fees](./trading/fees.md) for details.
